Contribution

Have you ever been using a terminal and thought, "Wow, it would be really convenient if I could ...?"
Now, with Web-Terminal, you can create this functionality!
Our goal is to create a terminal on training wheels, one that is as user friendly as possible, and with your help we can make this happen.

How Can I Contribute?

We here at Web-Terminal are proud to have members of the community contribute to our work. Before beginning, we ask that you read our Contribution Guide.

Where Do I Begin?

Now that you know how to contribute, where should you begin?

  1. If you're a beginner, you can check out these good first issues, which we've identified as good areas for newcomers to get involved with the project.

  2. Once you're more familiar with Web-Terminal and its code, feel free to try and tackle any of our open issues.

Just a reminder: whenever you begin working on a code contribution for any open issue, please add a brief comment to the issue to let us know you're working on it.

I have an improvement idea, but I don't have much experience...

As a new coder, you are likely experiencing the terminal difficulties which we are aiming to eliminate! Even if you are not sure how to implement your improvement idea, we would love to hear it.
Submit your idea by creating a new feature request issue!
